  7. "Advertised Price" is the price at which each Item(s) is offered for sale on the TLV website. For the initial 3 months of the sale, the "Advertised Price" of the Item(s) will be the price set forth in the Pricing Proposal or will be a price mutually agreed prior to commencement of the sale. Should an Item not sell during this initial 3-month period, the Advertised Price for the Item(s) will automatically be reduced by 30%.

  9. TLV uses Sales Events, Trade Discounts and Coupons to help drive sales of Items. These "Discounts” offered to prospective buyers range from 10-15%. For a sold Item(s) any Discounts from the Advertised Price will be shared between TLV and the Consignor.
  10. Item(s) made available for sale through TLV include the "Make-an-Offer" functionality. Make-anOffer allows prospective buyers to "Offer" to buy an Item at a price below the Advertised Price. If such an Offer is made the Consignor will then have the ability to "accept", "reject" or "counter" the Offer. Please note that Discounts will not be applied when Buyer is utilizing the Make an Offer feature.
  11. When the sale of an Item(s) is Completed TLV shall retain a commission of 40% of the Sale Price for its services. The "Sale Price" is the price paid by the Buyer for the Item(s). The "Net Sale Proceeds" to be received by the Consignor is calculated as the Sale Price net of transaction fees and TLV commission. The Net Sale Proceeds will be sent to the Consignor at the address provided within approximately 14 business days after the Item(s) sale has been Completed.
  12. This Consignment Agreement is for an "Initial Term" of 4-months. The Consignor may withdraw any Item(s) from the sale after this Initial Term by sending written notice of withdrawal to TLV prior to the end of the 4-months. Should no withdrawal request be received by TLV the sale will be extended for an additional 2-month term at the Advertised Price. Subsequent 2-month extensions will automatically continue unless either a withdrawal notification from the Consignor is received by TLV or TLV decides to end the the sale of the Item(s).
  13. If Consignor demands or requests for TLV to withdraw any Item(s) within 24 hours after the Photoshoot described in "3" above, Consignor shall pay a $50 per Item withdrawn Cancellation Fee. Thereafter Consignor shall pay TLV a Cancellation Fee equal to 40% of the Advertised Price of any Item(s) where there is a sale agreed with a Buyer that is cancelled by Consignor or where Item(s) is withdrawn by Consignor during the Initial Term. In such event TLV will charge the Consignor’s credit card provided herein or bill the Consignor for the Cancellation Fee.
